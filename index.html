Who We Are

WOKE! was founded in November 2017 by our executive chef Max Daniel and Kashmir Hughes, under the name Food with Love, inside of a small food bank in Sun Valley, CA with one mission; to serve fresh, healthy meals at a reasonable price with the ingredients that were available on hand. Over the last year, we've served thousands of quality meals at fast food prices, and provided hundreds hours of training to training to youth in the local community. Now we're taking your favorite menu items, keeping them full time, and taking our mission mobile!
